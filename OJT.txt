On-the-Job Training (OJT) in Full Stack Web Development (MERN Stack)

On-the-Job Training (OJT) is a practical training program that enables students to gain real-world industrial experience and apply the theoretical knowledge acquired during their academic studies.

It helps students understand professional work culture, improve technical skills, and prepare for future careers in the IT industry.

I completed my OJT at Code Insight Academy (Branch of Zazpi Tech Pvt. Ltd.) in the domain of Full Stack Web Development (MERN Stack).

The objective of this training was to understand the complete process of developing modern web applications using both frontend and backend technologies.

During the training, I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB.

I developed responsive web pages, created reusable React components, understood backend development, database connectivity, REST API development, and CRUD operations. I also learned how frontend and backend communicate to build complete web applications.

As part of the training, I worked on an AI Resume Builder project using the MERN Stack.

The project allowed users to enter personal information, educational details, technical skills, and work experience to generate a professional resume.

Through this project, I gained practical experience in frontend development, backend integration, database management, debugging, and responsive web design.

Apart from technical knowledge, the training also enhanced my communication skills, teamwork, problem-solving ability, time management, and professional attitude.

Working under the guidance of experienced mentors helped me understand industry standards and software development practices.

Overall, this OJT was a valuable learning experience that strengthened my technical knowledge, improved my confidence, and prepared me for future opportunities in Full Stack Web Development and the IT industry.
